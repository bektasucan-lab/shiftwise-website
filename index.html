FAQ
Common Questions
What is ShiftSense?+
ShiftSense is an AI-powered companion app for shift workers. It helps you manage your schedule, optimize sleep, track energy, and get personalized recommendations from Wise, your AI assistant.
What can Wise AI do?+
Wise is more than a chatbot — it's an AI agent. Ask about sleep, energy, or your schedule. Wise can also set alarms, create shift plans, and adjust your routine automatically.
Is ShiftSense free to use?+
Yes! Basic features including calendar, energy scores, and 3 AI messages are free. PRO unlocks unlimited Wise AI, detailed analytics, and all premium features with a 7-day free trial.
Is my health data safe?+
Absolutely. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell personal data. Energy scores are calculated on your device. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.
Is ShiftSense a medical device?+
No. ShiftSense provides wellness information based on shift work research. It's not a medical device and doesn't provide medical advice. Always consult your doctor for health decisions.
