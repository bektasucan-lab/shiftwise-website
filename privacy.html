Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 3, 2026

Studio Lunora ("we", "us", "our") operates ShiftSense, a shift work companion application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Display name (optional)
  • Authentication provider data (Apple ID or Google Sign-In)

1.2 Shift & Schedule Data

  • Shift schedules you create or import
  • Work patterns and rotation templates
  • Notes added to shifts

1.3 Health & Wellness Data

  • Sleep schedule data (manually entered or from Wise AI conversations)
  • Energy and fatigue scores (calculated locally on your device)
  • Alarm and notification preferences

1.3a Apple HealthKit Data

With your explicit permission, the app may access the following data from Apple HealthKit:

  • Sleep analysis (SLEEP_ASLEEP, SLEEP_IN_BED)
  • Heart rate variability (HRV)
  • Resting heart rate
  • Step count
  • Active energy burned

This data is used exclusively to improve sleep and energy predictions. HealthKit data is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties, in compliance with Apple's HealthKit guidelines.

You can revoke HealthKit access at any time through iOS Settings > Privacy > Health.

1.4 AI Conversation Data

  • Messages you send to Wise AI assistant
  • Wise AI responses
  • AI-generated recommendations and action results

1.5 Usage & Analytics Data

  • App usage patterns (screens visited, features used)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Purchase and subscription status

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and personalize the ShiftSense experience
  • Generate AI-powered shift recommendations and sleep plans
  • Calculate energy scores and fatigue predictions
  • Send shift reminders and alarm notifications
  • Process in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Improve our services and fix bugs
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. AI Data Processing

3.1 How Wise AI Works

Wise AI processes your shift data and conversation messages to provide personalized recommendations. Your messages are sent to our secure server (Supabase Edge Functions), which communicates with Anthropic's Claude AI models.

3.2 What We Send to AI

  • Your current message
  • Recent conversation context (last 10 messages)
  • Your shift schedule and work pattern
  • Your onboarding preferences (shift type, sleep habits)
What We Do NOT Send to AI: Your email address or real name, your financial or earnings data, your device information, or your purchase history.

3.3 AI Data Retention

Conversation history is stored in your Supabase account. You can delete your conversation history at any time. AI model providers (Anthropic) do not store your data beyond processing.

4. Data Storage & Security

4.1 Local Storage

Shift data, alarms, and preferences are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage (Hive). Local data is not accessible to other apps.

4.2 Cloud Storage

Account data and AI conversations are stored on Supabase (hosted on AWS). All data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest. Row Level Security (RLS) ensures you can only access your own data.

4.3 Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics for anonymous usage statistics and Firebase Crashlytics for crash reporting. These services collect anonymized device and usage data.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Anthropic — AI conversation processing (messages only, no personal identifiers)
  • Apple / Google — Authentication and in-app purchase processing
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management (purchase status only)
  • Firebase (Google) — Analytics and crash reporting (anonymized)
  • Supabase — Cloud data storage and authentication

6. Your Rights

6.1 Access & Export

You can view all your data within the app at any time. You can export your shift data and earnings reports as PDF files.

6.2 Correction

You can edit your display name and shift data at any time.

6.3 Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data from the Profile screen. This action is permanent and includes all shift data, AI conversation history, preferences, and your authentication account.
